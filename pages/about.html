Introduction to Traditionalist Philosophy

What is Traditionalism?

Traditionalism is a collection of ideas aiming to reestablish a hierarchy of morals, social class, ethics, and authority, as well as upholding metaphysical and hermetical values and principles and weaponise them against the postmodern globalist order and Liberalism.

Principles and Values

Militarism

Militarism is the glorification and prioritization of military values, objectives, and interests in a society or state

"Do not think that I came to bring peace on earth. I did not come to bring peace but a sword."- Matthew 10:34-36

Collectivism

Collectivism is a social, economic, and political philosophy that emphasizes the importance of collective action and the common good over the interests of individual members of a society

"And hold firmly to the rope of God and do not be divided. Remember God’s favour upon you when you were enemies, then He united your hearts, so you—by His grace—became brothers. And you were at the brink of a fiery pit and He saved you from it. This is how God makes His revelations clear to you, so that you may be guided."- Quran - 3:103

Religion

Religion is a set of beliefs, practices, and rituals that are concerned with the worship of a higher power or divine being, and that often involve the pursuit of moral and spiritual enlightenment

"My concern is not whether God is on our side; my greatest concern is to be on God's side, for God is always right."- Abraham Lincoln

Justice

Justice is a concept that encompasses the notions of fairness, righteousness, and moral rightness, and is often used to describe the principles and practices that govern the allocation of benefits, burdens, and obligations within a society

"O believers! Stand firm for justice as witnesses for God even if it is against yourselves, your parents, or close relatives. Be they rich or poor, God is best to ensure their interests. So do not let your desires cause you to deviate from justice. If you distort the testimony or refuse to give it, then know that God is certainly All-Aware of what you do."- Quran - 4:136

Aristocracy

Aristocracy is a form of government in which a small group of noble and privileged members of a social class hold a certain level of authority and power and influence over a nation, typically through religious status, wisdom, merit, knowledge and/or wealth.

"Until philosophers are kings, or the kings and princes of this world have the spirit and power of philosophy ... cities will never have rest from their evils,—no, nor the human race, as I believe,—and then only will this our State have a possibility of life and behold the light of day."- Socrates

Transcendence

Transcendence is the idea of achieving a state of being or consciousness that is beyond the limitations of the physical world or the ego

"The Perennial Philosophy is expressed most succinctly in the Sanskrit formula, tat tvam asi (‘That thou art’); the Atman, or immanent eternal Self, is one with Brahman, the Absolute Principle of all existence; and the last end of every human being, is to discover the fact for himself, to find out who he really is."- Aldous Huxley
